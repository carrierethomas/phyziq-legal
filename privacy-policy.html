Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 24, 2026

Phyziq ("we", "our", or "us") operates the Phyziq mobile application. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our app.

1. Information We Collect

Account Data: When you create an account, we collect your name, email address, and authentication credentials (via Apple Sign-In or Google Sign-In). We use Supabase as our backend provider to store this data securely.

Body Photos and Scan Data: When you use the body scanning feature, we collect photos you take or upload. These photos are transmitted to Anthropic (Claude AI) for morphological analysis to generate body composition estimates including body fat percentage, muscle mass distribution, and morphological traits. No photo is stored by Anthropic — only the analysis results are saved in your Phyziq account. You will be asked for your explicit consent before any photo is sent for analysis.

Health and Fitness Data: We collect fitness-related information you provide, such as your fitness goals, training preferences, workout history, and body measurements over time.

Payment Data: Subscription payments are processed by Apple (App Store) and managed through RevenueCat. We do not directly collect or store your credit card information. RevenueCat receives transaction receipts to validate your subscription status.

Analytics Data: We may collect usage analytics including app interactions, feature usage, and device identifiers to improve our service. This data is processed by Mixpanel.

Device Information: We may collect device type, operating system version, and unique device identifiers for app functionality and analytics.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide the body scanning and AI analysis features
  • To generate personalized training programs based on your scan results and goals
  • To track your fitness progress over time
  • To manage your subscription and provide premium features
  • To improve our app and develop new features
  • To send you notifications about your training programs (with your consent)
  • To provide customer support

3. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell your personal information. We share data only with the following service providers who are necessary for app functionality:

  • Anthropic: AI analysis provider (Claude API) — your body photos are transmitted solely for morphological analysis and are not retained by Anthropic after processing. See Anthropic's Privacy Policy.
  • Supabase: Backend infrastructure and data storage
  • RevenueCat: Subscription management and payment validation
  • Mixpanel: Analytics (you can opt out of analytics tracking)
  • Apple: Authentication (Sign in with Apple) and payment processing

Each provider processes data in accordance with their own privacy policies and our data processing agreements.

4. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely using Supabase with row-level security policies ensuring that only you can access your own data. All data is transmitted over HTTPS. We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information.

Body scan photos are stored securely and are only accessible to your account. We strip EXIF and location metadata from photos before processing.

5. Data Retention and Deletion

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. You can delete your account and all associated data at any time directly within the app (Settings - Delete Account). Upon account deletion, we will remove all your personal data, scan photos, and fitness data from our systems within 30 days.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Delete your account and data
  • Export your data in a machine-readable format
  • Withdraw consent for analytics tracking at any time
  • Opt out of marketing communications

7. GDPR Compliance (European Users)

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA), we process your data based on the following legal bases: your consent (for analytics, optional features, and AI photo analysis), contractual necessity (for core app functionality), and legitimate interest (for security and service improvement). You may exercise your rights under GDPR by contacting us at the email below.

8. Children's Privacy

Phyziq is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we learn that we have collected data from a child under 13, we will delete that information promptly.

9. Health Disclaimer

Phyziq is not a medical device. Body composition estimates and fitness recommendations are for informational purposes only and should not be considered medical advice. Always consult a healthcare professional before making health or fitness decisions.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by updating the "Last updated" date and, where appropriate, providing notice within the app.
